The Superior Choice

LumiNeon vs. Glass Neon

Why modern architectural-grade LED flex has completely revolutionized the custom lighting industry.

LumiNeon LED Flex

The Modern Standard

  • Shatterproof

    Built with flexible silicone and acrylic. Safe for high-traffic areas, kids, and pets.

  • Energy Efficient

    Consumes 80% less power. Safe 12V system keeps energy bills exceptionally low.

  • Fully Controllable

    Comes with a remote to adjust brightness, switch modes, or strobe to the beat.

  • Affordable Pricing

    Significantly lower upfront costs and virtually zero maintenance expenses over its 50k hour lifespan.

Traditional Glass

The Outdated Method

  • Highly Fragile

    Made of brittle glass tubes filled with toxic gas. Extremely dangerous if broken.

  • High Voltage & Heat

    Runs very hot, making it unsafe to touch. Emits an audible buzzing sound.

  • No Dimming

    Always on at 100% brightness. Cannot be dimmed or controlled remotely.

  • Expensive Upkeep

    Very expensive to purchase, requires specialized installers, and frequently needs gas refills.

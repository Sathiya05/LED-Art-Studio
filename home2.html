How It's Made

1

Design & Mockup

Submit your idea. We create a digital blueprint and send a rendering for your great approval.

2

Routing Acrylic

Industrial CNC machines precisely cut the high-density acrylic backing to your exact shape.

3

LED light Flexing

Artisans hand-bend the silicone LED strips, fitting them perfectly into the routed channels.

4

Burn-In and Ship

24-hour continuous light test ensures perfect quality before secure packaging and global dispatch.

Our Story

LumiNeon started in a small garage in 2018 with a simple obsession: bending light to create art that speaks. Frustrated by the fragile nature of traditional glass neon and the cheap look of mass-produced LEDs, we set out to bridge the gap.

Today, we are a collective of designers, fabricators, and engineers building premium, durable, and striking LED neon signs. Every piece that leaves our studio is handcrafted, tested rigorously, and designed to turn heads.

Our Mission

To democratize custom lighting design by providing accessible, high-quality, and eco-friendly LED neon art that empowers individuals and brands to express their unique identity.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for modern neon aesthetics, pushing the boundaries of what is possible with light, high-form technology, and sustainable materials in interior spaces.

The Art of Craftsmanship

From a digital sketch to a glowing reality, our process is meticulous.

01.

Digital Precision

Every sign starts in our design lab. We trace, adjust, and map out the exact paths for the LED nodes, ensuring perfect typography and structural integrity.

02.

Hand Fabrication

Our master fabricators route the acrylic backing and manually bend the silicone LED tubing, securing it flawlessly to match the design blueprint.

03.

Testing & QA

Before shipping, every sign undergoes a 24-hour burn-in test to ensure color consistency, brightness, and electrical safety.
